Team Captain App

Organizing the details so you can focus on the game

The Problem

Organizing a pickup soccer league takes coordination. Teams needs to quickly know their game schedule, communicate with their entire team, know who from their team will not be available to play, as well as make sure that everyone is up to date with their league fees.

My Solution

Team Captain app funtions as a platform for local soccer leagues to communicate with their teammates, check game schedules, see who is available to play and manage paying league fees. Players can update their status to indicate their weekly availabilty and message the entire team or individual players.





1st User: Logs in, checks schedule, sends message to entire team

George Wright, checks his teams Schedule to remind himself what team they will be playing this weekend. He is excited to find they will be playing the Hammer Heads. He then checks his team status and notices that his Center Defender, Derick, has not yet paid his fee to be a part of the league. he also notices the majority of his team has not specified if they will be available to play this weekend. George decides to send out a friendly reminder to the entire team to ask that those who have not yet made their payments do so ASAP. He also decides to include a reminder for all player to let him know whether or not they will be able to make it to the game this weekend.





2nd User: Checks Messages, changes availability status and sends DM to team captain

Derek logs into his account and receives a notification that he has an unread message team captain. He navigates to messages and sees that his captian, George, has sent out a message to the entire team. He reads the message remembers he has not marked is availabilty for this week yet and is slightly embarrassed to realize that he has not yet made his payments to play. He navigates to his profile to indicate he is available. He then marks his payment status as paid as he plans to Venmo his captain after closing the app. He then sends his captain a direct message and lets him know that he is sending the money now.
