goodreads Redesign

A redesign of Goodread's "My Books" page

The Problem

This project aimed to give the Goodreads' "My Books" page a facelift. I aimed to make the UX slightly more intuitive and wanted to spruce up the sight to create more of a modern and comfortable feel.

Solution

The content of the page, including filtering and sorting options, remained on the page. Modeling after the iTunes library layout, I aimed to create a new page that feels modern and familiar.

Redesign and Rebranding

In my redesign I wanted to create the feeling of being in a small, comfortable reading knook. It was important to me that the logo reflect the feeling of getting lost in a book. After playing around with different font types I then dove into creating a image to tie in the ideas of imagination and cozy comfort. I settled on the idea of a child sitting and reading to inspire the idea that reading has the power to bring out our childlike imagination.

I choose colors that created a bright, cheerful and safe feeling.
